Hero's of planet earth(H.O.P.E) Your smart climate buddy to help you do your bit for the climate. A committed effort to bring awareness about climate change and how your small effort can serve the greater cause. It is designed in the form of a interactive social game that challenges you to complete tasks of varying difficulty based on your current location and time. It lets you do your bit to help the environment with the resources at hand and makes it a fun filled activity by letting you nominate friends, challenge them, maintain leaderboards and so on.

So, start taking the challenges and upload your selfie or video of you doing the challenge. Points will be given based upon the challenge.

There are 2 kinds of challenges

a)Weekly Challenge - This is a big challenge and would require more time. Don't worry,more points are rewarded if this challenge is completed.

b)Mini Challenges - These set of challenges are those that can be done on a day to day basis.One challenge is generated depending upon your location and timeand the the rest are static

So get started and give H.O.P.E to society!!
