Mini Challenges

1.Collect the water you see while rinsing fruits and vegetables.Use it to water plants

So,every time you rinse vegetables or anything,we end up using utensils which has pores and inturn loose the water.Instead it can be collected and be used to water the plants.At the end of the day we efficiently save water and contribute with personal incentives.




2.When doing laundry match the size of the water to the size of the load

Just ensure that while soaking the clothes in a bucket or a tub before loading them into washing machine that water is not filled up till the neck but with the amount that is required with respect to the load.




3.Turn off the water while brushing

By doing this everyday, we can make a big difference and save upto gallons of water.
